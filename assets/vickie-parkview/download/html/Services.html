Healthcare services

Take charge of your health with high-quality care, right here at home.

Whether you’re planning for an upcoming procedure or scheduling your annual wellness visit, Parkview Logansport Hospital is committed to providing unparalleled—inpatient and outpatient—healthcare services to help you feel your best.

We are focused on serving our community where they work, live, and play. We want you to feel confident in the care that you receive while you’re here with us because we are passionate about providing quality care for the families and residents of north-central Indiana.
